About Us

Welcome to AUN

Founded by Obillaneni Karthik Sri and spearheaded by Sankeerth Kohirkar as the Co-founder and CEO, AUN ALL U NEED is a pioneering startup dedicated to revolutionizing the way students access groceries and essentials in college campuses located in the outskirts.

Our Mission

At AUN, our mission is simple yet profound: to bridge the gap in access to essential goods for students in colleges situated in remote areas. We envision a future where every student has convenient access to quality groceries and daily necessities, regardless of their geographical location.

Empowering Student Life

Understanding the challenges faced by students in accessing basic amenities, we have tailored our services to cater specifically to their needs. Whether it's textbooks, stationery, snacks, or other essentials, AUN ALL U NEED is committed to providing a seamless and reliable solution.

Why Choose Us?

Student-Centric Approach

Our curated selection of products is carefully chosen to meet the diverse needs of college students, ensuring convenience and affordability.

Innovative Delivery Model:

Through strategic bulk deliveries at regular intervals, we optimize logistics and pass on the savings to our customers, making everyday essentials more accessible than ever.

Community Engagement:

Beyond just a delivery service, AUN ALL U NEED serves as a platform for students to connect, share recommendations, and foster a sense of community within their campus.
Our Commitment to Sustainability

AUN ALL U NEED is not just about convenience; it's also about responsibility. We are committed to sustainable practices, offering eco-friendly packaging options and minimizing our environmental footprint wherever possible.

Join us in our mission to empower student life and redefine convenience in college campuses. Experience the AUN ALL U NEED difference today!
